Retiring Unused Functions

As functions age, they may no longer be used. The one caveat is than an 'unused function' may be a command-line function. Given an othewise unused function, here's a process, a work-flow to retire a function.

After updating Function Libraries

A typical example

set -- cqdatalib rdlib

fun_dupsfm0 $1 retiring

fun_dupsfm1 $1 retiring

any of the steps may be preceeded by on or off, enabling or disabling debugging.

Soucre the updated libraries

When doing a wrap_up from the devdir, it may list one or more library names at the end. These are those who've been moved to retiring

source ../cqdatalib source ../rdlib

fbdy $* | tee -a ../retiring cd ..

off; fun_dupsfm1 rdlib retiring

mv nextlib rdlib

mv nextlib cqdatalib wrap_up
